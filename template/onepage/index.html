OUR STORY

We were introduced through shaadi, a matchmaking service. Tejasvi was introduced to Rohith by her father, Lakshman Samala. Rohith spoke with Teja on Jan 8th, 2021, for the first time for about 58min over the phone. We talked a lot over Instagram and fell in love with each other just by text messages. Rohith was in India for a Vacation and Flew from India to New Jersey to meet the gorgeous lady Tejasvi Samala for the first time on Feb 8th, 2021. It took several days and significant other before we could see that we belonged together. Our love story started with a spark to get to where we are today, and we couldn’t be happier. We both got engaged on Feb 13th, 2021. The best gift Rohith could offer Tejasvi on her birthday was a Love Ring asking for Marriage. It was exhilarating and spontaneous, and we both knew right then that no matter how many miles were between us or however many days we had to be apart. We wanted to be together for the rest of our lives. We can’t wait to celebrate our wedding day with you!
